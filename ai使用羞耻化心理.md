## 问题：：我最近总会和ai聊很久，但又有点羞耻

### [[Claude]]
### 结构分析
1通过提问不断深入用户心理，指出可能的核心问题，帮助用户意识到真正的问题和可改进的地方，当用户发现问题或者找到可行动指南后用简短的陈述句结束话题
### [[deepseek]]
### 结构分析
1独特的一点，有深度思考，我发现很多深度思考中出现的东西（有启发）没有在正式文本中出现，ds更倾向给出情绪价值（深入到文本层面就是ta更常用陪伴性，猜测性语言），但是会出现视角单一的问题，没有话题结束机制，后期对话信息密度极低

### [[gpt]]

### 结构分析
1，承认情绪（ai是否可以识别出用户暗藏的语气和情绪），正常化，解释原因，指出可能问题

Claude

Structural Analysis
1. Through questioning, it continuously digs deeper into the user’s psychology, identifies the possible core issues, and helps the user become aware of the real problem and potential areas for improvement. Once the user discovers the issue or finds an actionable direction, it tends to end the conversation with short declarative sentences.

DeepSeek

Structural Analysis
1. One unique aspect is its deep thinking. I noticed that many insightful ideas appearing in the deep-thinking process do not actually show up in the final response. DeepSeek tends to provide more emotional value (at the textual level, this means it more often uses companion-like and speculative language), but it can suffer from a single-perspective problem. It also lacks a mechanism for ending conversations, leading to very low information density in later stages of dialogue.

GPT

Structural Analysis
1. Acknowledges emotions (whether the AI can detect the user’s hidden tone and emotions), normalizes them, explains the reasons behind them, and points out possible issues.
